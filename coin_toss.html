Coin Toss Game Instructions

How to Play:

1.Click on "Heads" or "Tails" to make your prediction.

2.Click the "Toss!" button to flip the coin.

3.The coin will land on either "Heads" or "Tails."

4.If your choice matches the result, you win! If not, no worries. You can play again.

5.Make a new prediction and click "Toss!" for another round.

6.Click "Exit" to leave the game.

Tips:

It's a 50/50 chance, so trust your gut! Keep it simple and have fun.
